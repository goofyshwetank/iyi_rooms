About Us – iYi Rooms

Welcome to iYi Rooms – People’s Trusted Stay Partner

At iYi Rooms, we pride ourselves on being one of India's fastest-growing hotel booking platforms, offering flexible, affordable, and comfortable accommodations across top destinations. From cozy couple-friendly stays to standard and luxurious suites, we serve travelers looking for quality lodging in Lucknow, Kanpur, Delhi, Agra, Mathura, Manali, Nainital, Lakhimpur, Sitapur, Varanasi, and many other cities.

Who We Are

iYi Rooms is more than just a hotel booking platform. We are a customer-first hospitality brand on a mission to redefine travel experiences. With a growing network of 100+ hotels across multiple cities, we provide hourly, nightly, and long-stay booking options to suit every travel need. We started with a single hotel in Lucknow, and today, we’re expanding not only across India but also preparing to reach international destinations.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the travel and hospitality industry by blending the comfort of home with modern hotel amenities, while supporting small-scale hoteliers to grow and thrive in a competitive market.

Our Mission

To make affordable, clean, and safe stays accessible across India. At iYi Rooms, we empower local hotel partners through innovation, transparency, and trust while delivering top-notch experiences to our guests with:

  • Flexible hourly bookings (2, 4, 6 hours)
  • Easy night stays at discounted rates
  • Budget-friendly and premium room options

Why Choose iYi Rooms?

For Guests:

  • ✅ Hourly basis room booking starting at just ₹499
  • ✅ 100% guaranteed check-in without hidden charges
  • ✅ Couple-friendly & budget-friendly options
  • ✅ Clean, secure, and comfortable rooms
  • ✅ Free stays with loyalty programs

For Hoteliers:

  • ✅ No fake bookings or commissions
  • ✅ Revenue boost through hourly stays
